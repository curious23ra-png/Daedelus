🚀 OVERVIEW

DAEDALUS is an autonomous cryptocurrency trading intelligence platform designed for Coinbase Advanced Trade. It combines a real-time market scanner, self-evolving AI strategy (DNA), paper trading simulator, live order execution, and gold price monitoring — all from a single, self-contained browser app with no server dependencies.

Trades are placed through your Coinbase API key. All configuration, trade history, and AI state persist in your browser's localStorage — they survive page reloads and browser restarts automatically.

⚠️ Important: DAEDALUS executes real trades with real money when Autopilot is running in LIVE mode. Always test with Paper Mode first. The developer accepts no liability for trading losses.
⚡ GETTING STARTED
  1. Open Settings (⚙️ tab) and enter your Coinbase Advanced Trade API Key and Secret. Keys must have Trade + View permissions. Keys are stored only in your browser.
  2. Enable Paper Mode first (toggle in Settings → DCA card or Paper tab). This lets you test the full system without risking real funds.
  3. Configure Trade Sizing — set your USDC trade amount per position, max open positions, and scan limit.
  4. Run a Market Scan on the Market Scan tab to preview buy signals before enabling autopilot.
  5. Start Autopilot from the Dashboard toolbar (▶ START BOT) or the Autopilot tab once you are comfortable with the paper results.
🗂️ TAB REFERENCE
⬡ DASHBOARD
Your command centre. Displays live account balances (CAD + USDC), open positions count, active buy signals from the last scan, and the real-time system log. The top toolbar gives one-click access to START BOT, EVOLVE DNA, and STOP. A live feed of buy signals detected in the most recent scan appears on the left; system events stream on the right.
◎ MARKET SCAN
Manually scan up to 200 USD-paired crypto assets on Coinbase for buy signals. Each pair is evaluated using RSI, price momentum, volume spikes, and the current DNA strategy parameters. Results are scored and ranked — green rows are BUY signals, red rows are SELL or neutral. Click BUY on any row to place an instant manual order, or let Autopilot act automatically. Adjust the Limit field to control how many pairs are scanned per cycle.
🤖 AUTOPILOT
Configure and control the autonomous trading engine. Set your per-trade USDC amount, maximum concurrent open positions, stop-loss %, and take-profit %. Click ▶ START AUTOPILOT to engage. The engine will scan the market, identify high-confidence signals matching the current DNA, execute buys, and manage exits automatically. Session stats (buys, sells, balances) are shown in real time.

🧠 Negative Position Learning Directive: Each bot cycle, if all open positions are simultaneously underwater, the engine automatically adds +1 slot by buying the top-scoring available market signal not already held — hedging the drawdown with the strongest current momentum asset. This event is logged as 🧠 DIRECTIVE in the system log and counted in the DNA knowledge base to influence future evolution.
🧬 STRATEGY DNA
DAEDALUS uses a self-evolving parameter set called DNA. Each generation contains entry thresholds (RSI oversold, drop velocity, bounce confirmation, volume spike), exit thresholds (min profit %, stop-loss %, RSI overbought, trailing stop), and DCA rules. After every evolution cycle (default: 4 hours), the engine analyses recent trade outcomes and mutates the DNA toward more profitable parameters. The Evolution Log shows every generation change. You can force an immediate evolution with ⚡ FORCE EVOLVE, save the current DNA, or reset to factory defaults.
📂 POSITIONS
Shows all currently open live positions with entry price, current live price, and unrealised P&L. Each row includes a mini sparkline chart — click it to instantly open the full interactive chart for that asset (candle, line, or bar view with full time-range controls). Click ↻ REFRESH to sync with Coinbase. Positions with exits triggered (by stop-loss, take-profit, or trailing stop) are automatically closed by Autopilot. In Paper Mode, positions are simulated and shown here as well.
📋 HISTORY
A log of all trades executed in the current session (buy/sell pairs, amounts, generation number, timestamps, and Coinbase order IDs). History is session-only and clears on page refresh. For persistent records, note your order IDs from the Coinbase portal.
✋ MANUAL TRADE
Place a one-off buy or sell order instantly. Enter the trading pair (e.g. BTC-CAD), choose BUY or SELL, set the CAD amount, and click ⚡ EXECUTE. Also houses the CAD → USDC Converter widget, which lets you convert your available CAD balance to USDC for use as trading capital.
⭐ WATCHLIST
Monitor specific assets at a glance. Add any Coinbase-traded pair to your watchlist and DAEDALUS will display live price, 24h change, RSI, and current signal score. Price threshold alerts can be set per asset — you'll receive an in-app toast notification when a threshold is breached.
🥇 GOLD MONITOR
Tracks the live spot price of gold in both USD and CAD using multiple price sources (CoinGecko PAXG, metals.live, goldprice.org). Set a BUY threshold (price floor) and SELL threshold (price ceiling) in CAD. When gold crosses either level, DAEDALUS rings a phone-call style alert you can Answer (acknowledge) or Decline (silence until the price resets). Declined alerts re-arm automatically once the price re-enters range. Auto-refresh fetches a new price every 5 minutes when enabled. A Wealthsimple reference price field lets you track the spread between the live gold price and your WS purchase price.
📄 PAPER TRADING
Full simulation of the live trading engine using a virtual balance. Paper trades are executed at live market prices but no real orders are placed. Use Paper Mode to test strategy configurations, DNA parameters, and Autopilot behaviour safely. Paper stats (wins, losses, P&L, win rate) are tracked separately and persist across sessions. Wipe paper data at any time via Settings → Danger Zone or the WIPE button on this tab.
🤖 ROBOCOP
An autonomous self-diagnostic module that monitors the app in real time for API errors, failed price fetches, stale data, and signal logic faults. When a fault is detected Robocop diagnoses the root cause and applies an auto-fix without requiring a restart. Enable via the toggle in the Robocop tab or the Robocop switch in the top toolbar. The Diagnostic Feed and Fix History logs show every event caught and every patch applied.
⚙️ SETTINGS
Central control panel for all DAEDALUS parameters. Organised into cards: Autopilot Control (cycle intervals), Trade Sizing, Exit Thresholds (take-profit, stop-loss, trailing stop, urgency ramp, target hold time), DCA (averaging-down rules), DNA Entry/Exit signals, and the Danger Zone (factory reset, DNA reset, data wipe). All settings persist in localStorage. Always click the ✅ APPLY button within each card to confirm changes.
🧬 DNA PARAMETERS EXPLAINED
── ENTRY SIGNALS ──

Min Signal Score: Minimum composite score a pair must reach before a buy is placed. Higher = fewer, higher-confidence trades.

RSI Oversold: RSI level below which a pair is considered oversold (buy-biased). Default 55 — lowering increases selectivity.

Drop Velocity: Minimum % drop speed needed to qualify as a bounce setup. Prevents buying into slow bleeds.

Bounce Confirm %: Price must tick up by this % from the local bottom to confirm a reversal.

Volume Spike Min: Volume must be this multiple of recent average to confirm institutional interest.

── EXIT SIGNALS ──

Min Profit % (DNA): Minimum unrealised gain before the exit logic even considers selling.

Stop Loss % (DNA): Unrealised loss at which the position is automatically closed.

RSI Overbought: RSI above this level signals a potential top — triggers sell consideration.

Trailing Stop %: Once a position is profitable, lock in gains by closing if price drops this % from the peak.

Trail Activate At %: Trailing stop only arms once the position reaches this % profit.

📉 DCA — AVERAGING DOWN

DCA (Dollar-Cost Averaging) allows DAEDALUS to buy additional units of an asset when a position moves against you, lowering your average entry price.

DCA Trigger Drop %: If a position drops by this %, an additional buy order is placed.

DCA Max Rounds: Maximum number of DCA additions per position. Set to 0 to fully disable DCA.

DCA Size Multiplier: Each DCA buy is this multiple of the previous buy size (e.g. 2.0 = double each round).

⚠️ DCA increases your total exposure per position. Use conservatively and ensure your account has sufficient CAD/USDC reserves.
🎯 EXIT MECHANICS

DAEDALUS uses a layered exit system. All conditions are checked every Exit Check Interval seconds (default 8s).

  1. Take Profit: Close when unrealised gain ≥ Take Profit % setting.
  2. Stop Loss: Close when unrealised loss ≥ Stop Loss % setting.
  3. Trailing Stop: Once profit ≥ Trail Activate At %, a trailing stop arms at Trail Stop % below the peak price. If price drops back through the trail, position is closed to lock in gains.
  4. Target Hold Time + Urgency Ramp: After the target hold time (default 5 min), the urgency ramp begins lowering the profit threshold toward the Urgency Floor %, forcing an exit even at minimal gain to free up capital.
  5. DNA Exit Signals: RSI overbought + peak velocity + exhaustion drop pattern can trigger a sell regardless of profit level, if the DNA deems momentum spent.
🥇 GOLD MONITOR — DETAILED GUIDE

The Gold Monitor fetches spot gold prices from multiple sources in order of reliability: CoinGecko (PAXG token price), metals.live, and goldprice.org. All prices are displayed in both USD and CAD. CAD conversion uses the live Frankfurter FX rate with a hard fallback.

Alert Thresholds:

  • BUY Threshold (Low): Alert fires if gold CAD price drops to or below this level — signals a potential buy opportunity.
  • SELL Threshold (High): Alert fires if gold CAD price rises to or above this level — signals a potential sell opportunity.
  • Drop Alert %: Alert fires if gold drops by this % in a single refresh cycle.

Call-Style Alerts: When a threshold is breached, a full-screen "incoming call" overlay appears with ringtone. Answer to acknowledge (alert can fire again next cycle if price stays at the level). Decline to silence that specific threshold until the price resets back into the normal range.

Wealthsimple Reference: Enter your WS gold purchase price per gram to track the live spread and decide when to switch platforms for a better rate.

🤖 ROBOCOP DIRECTIVE

Robocop is DAEDALUS's internal watchdog. Once active, it runs a diagnostic scan every 30 seconds checking for:

  • API fetch failures or timeouts
  • Stale price data (data not updated recently)
  • Scanner producing zero results unexpectedly
  • Balance sync errors or invalid balance reads
  • Log overflow or UI element absence

When a fault is detected Robocop attempts an automatic fix (e.g. re-triggers a balance fetch, forces a re-scan, clears a stuck log). All actions are logged in the Diagnostic Feed and Fix History.

Enable Robocop via the Robocop tab toggle or the 🤖 switch in the top toolbar. You can force an immediate diagnostic scan at any time with ⚡ FORCE SCAN.

💡 TIPS & BEST PRACTICES
  • Always start in Paper Mode. Run at least 24–48 hours of paper trades to evaluate the current DNA before switching to live.
  • Keep trade amounts small relative to your balance. 1–5% of available USDC per trade is a sensible starting range.
  • Let the DNA evolve. The default evolution interval is 4 hours. After several generations, the strategy self-optimises to recent market conditions.
  • Use the Watchlist to monitor your highest-conviction assets without running a full scan every time.
  • Enable Robocop whenever Autopilot is running — it catches silent failures that can otherwise go unnoticed.
  • DCA carefully. A 2× multiplier with 3 rounds means your 4th position is 8× your base trade size. Ensure your account can support this before markets move against you.
  • Back up your DNA. Use the 💾 SAVE button in the DNA tab to note your best-performing generation parameters before forcing an evolution.
🔒 DATA & PRIVACY

DAEDALUS is a fully client-side application. All data — API keys, DNA parameters, trade history, watchlist, gold alert settings — is stored exclusively in your browser's localStorage. Nothing is transmitted to any third-party server except the Coinbase API and price data sources.

Price data is fetched from public APIs: CoinGecko, CryptoCompare, metals.live, goldprice.org, and Frankfurter FX. No personal data is sent to these services.

Warning: Clearing your browser data or using private/incognito mode will wipe all stored settings and history. Export or note your key parameters before clearing browser storage.
